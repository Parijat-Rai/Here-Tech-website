Frequently Asked Questions

The most common questions regarding the competetion.

Q1. Why is Comp-a-Thon different? Comp-a-thon is not a normal hackathon event but instead the real life cycle of how big tech companies/High growth startups develop their products and what they look for while recruiting new talents.
Q2. What will be the flow of the event? The event will be split into 3 days over the weekend, beginning on friday (9th September) with the pitching session, then moving onto the mentoring session, the wild card entries, product mapping and ending with the final development on Sunday(11th September). A timeline with complete details shall be released soon.
Q3. Why should you participate? Well the reasons are infinite but to name a few ones:
1. Guidance on how to crack technical interviews
2. Getting a 1-on-1 mentoring with our Mentors
3. A chance to get referrals to the MNCs and Startups
4. Cash prices!!
5. Goodies
6. Direct Internship opportunities in certain startups
7. Getting trading advice from the BU startups and indicator access for 2 months
Q4. Who are the mentors? Our Mentors will be the students who have gone through the same process and now are working in the Big Tech companies and High growth startups.
Q5. When do registrations end? Registrations close on the 6th of September at 2 pm. All interested participants must be registered before the deadline as no registrations shall be accepted afterwards.
